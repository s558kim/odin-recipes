Sourdough Bread

Ingredients

  • 150g/ 5.35 oz bubbly, active starter
  • 250g/ 8.80 oz warm water, preferably filtered*
  • 25g/ .90 oz olive oil
  • 500g/ 17.65 oz bread flour (not all purpose flour)
  • 10g/ .4 oz fine sea salt
  • 3 eggs
  • fine ground cornmeal, for dusting

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Bulk rise
  3. Optional step: Stretch & Fold the Dough
  4. Cut & Shape the Dough.
  5. Second Rise
  6. Score the Dough
  7. Bake the Dough
